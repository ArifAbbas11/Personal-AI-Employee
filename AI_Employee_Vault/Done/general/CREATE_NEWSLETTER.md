# Create Weekly Newsletter

Create a weekly newsletter for our subscribers covering recent developments.

## Requirements

1. **Topic**: AI automation and productivity tools
2. **Length**: 300-400 words
3. **Sections**:
   - Introduction
   - Key highlights (3-4 items)
   - Practical tip
   - Call to action

4. **Tone**: Professional but friendly
5. **Target audience**: Business professionals interested in AI

## Output

Create draft newsletter and place in Pending_Approval/ for review.

## Success Criteria

- Draft created with all required sections
- Word count within range
- Ready for human approval
